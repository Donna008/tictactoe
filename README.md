### Game Instruction:
    * Board Display:
          1 | 2 | 3 
         -----------
          4 | 5 | 6 
         -----------
          7 | 8 | 9 
         -----------
    
    * How to play the game
        Step 1: enter the command in the console: dotnet run
        Step 2: enter a cell number(1-9) in the console
        Step 3: keep going step 2 until game over
        Step 4: have fun
